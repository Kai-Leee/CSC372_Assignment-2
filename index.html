Restaurants On Campus

Food and drink near Petty Building.

Introduction

This blog introduces my recommended food spots within a half-mile walking distance from UNCG’s Petty Building.

These places are convenient, student-friendly, and great for a quick meal between classes.

Some of the dishes listed are ones I have personally tried and enjoyed.

Others are highly rated and popular among students in the area.

Extra Info

If you go to Don, you might meet the Korean owner—super friendly and a fun part of the experience.

There’s also a hidden coffee spot nearby with surprisingly good drinks. Want to go find it together sometime?

Tip: Going a little earlier than peak lunch time can help you avoid long lines and find seating faster.

Restaurants

Don

Address: 423 Tate St, Greensboro, NC 27403
Specialties: Spicy Ramen, Donburi, Spicy Tuna Don

Quick Japanese comfort food near campus. Great for a warm meal between classes.

Spicy Ramen

Rich broth with a spicy kick. Perfect for colder days.

Spicy Tuna Don

Fresh tuna over rice with a flavorful spicy sauce.

Donburi

A comforting Japanese rice bowl topped with savory ingredients.

Crazy Mexico

Address: 332 Tate St, Greensboro, NC 27403
Specialties: Tacos, Seafood plates, Mexican desserts

A colorful Mexican restaurant on Tate Street known for generous portions and authentic flavors.

Fried Ice Cream

Crispy outside, creamy inside. A fun and sweet treat.

Whole Fried Tilapia

Served with rice, salad, and avocado. Big portion and flavorful.

Tacos Plate

Classic street-style tacos with fresh toppings.

Chipotle

Address: 435 Tate St, Greensboro, NC 27403
Specialties: Burritos, Bowls, Tacos

Popular fast-casual Mexican grill where you can customize your burritos and bowls.

Burrito Bowl

A customizable bowl with rice, beans, protein, and toppings.

Chicken Burrito

Wrapped tortilla packed with seasoned chicken and fresh salsa.

Chips & Guacamole

Freshly made guacamole served with crispy tortilla chips.
